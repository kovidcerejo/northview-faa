Volunteer Your Time

Volunteer with the FAA

A few hours of your time makes a huge difference for student artists. Have fun, meet great people, and support the programs your kids love.

Why Volunteer?

Beyond the impact, volunteering is genuinely a good time.

Direct Impact

Every dollar raised at concession stands goes straight to the Art, Band, Chorus, Drama, and Orchestra programs.

Community

Meet other fine arts parents who share your passion for keeping the arts strong at Northview.

Low Commitment

Most volunteer shifts are just 2–3 hours. Sign up for one game or the whole season — your choice.

Flexible Scheduling

Sign up only for the dates that work for you. No minimum required, no pressure.

Current Volunteer Opportunities

Recurring

Football Game Concession Stands

2–3 hours per game

Run the concession stands at Northview Varsity and JV home football games. This is the FAA's primary fundraiser — and one of the most fun ways to get involved. You'll be working alongside other FAA parents, cheering on the school, and making a direct impact on the arts programs.

  • All training provided — no experience needed
  • Varsity and JV home games throughout the season
  • Work alongside fellow fine arts parents
  • All proceeds support the five arts programs
