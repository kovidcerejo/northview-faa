Get Involved with the FAA

Volunteering with us and linking Kroger Community Rewards are two great ways to get involved!

Why Volunteer?

Direct Impact

Sometimes the only reason a school event happens is because a parent volunteer said, "I've got this."

Community

Meet other fine arts parents who share your passion for keeping the arts strong at Northview.

Low Commitment

Most volunteer shifts are just 2–3 hours. Sign up for one game or the whole season — your choice.

Flexible Scheduling

Sign up only for the dates that work for you. No minimum required, no pressure.

Current Volunteer Opportunities

Recurring

Football Game Concession Stands

2–3 hours per game

Run the concession stands at Northview Varsity and JV home football games. This is the FAA's primary fundraiser — and one of the most fun ways to get involved. You'll be working alongside other FAA parents, cheering on the school, and making a direct impact on the arts programs.

  • All training provided — no experience needed
  • Varsity and JV home games throughout the season
  • Work alongside fellow fine arts parents
  • All proceeds support the five arts programs

Kroger Community Rewards

If you shop at Kroger, you can support the FAA every time you check out — at absolutely no cost to you. Just link your Kroger Plus card once and Kroger donates on your behalf.

Link My Kroger Card
1

Create or Sign In to Your Kroger Account

Visit krogercommunityrewards.com and sign in with your Kroger account, or create a free account if you don't have one.

2

Search for Northview FAA Programs

Under 'Community Rewards', search for any of the five Northview programs by name or by their code (listed below).

3

Select Your Program(s)

Select the program(s) you want to support. You can link multiple programs to support all the arts at once.

4

Shop as Usual

That's it! Every qualifying Kroger purchase you make will now earn a donation to the FAA at no extra cost to you.
