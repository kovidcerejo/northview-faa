Four Ways to Support

It takes a village to raise an artist. Here is how you can help our programs flourish.

1. Become a Member

Join the FAA starting at just $50/year. Your tax-deductible donation directly funds instruments, repairs, and equipment.

View Membership Tiers

2. Volunteer Your Time

Run concession stands at Northview Varsity and JV football home games. It's fun, easy, and incredibly helpful.

3. Link Your Kroger Card

It's completely free for you! Link your Kroger Plus card to one of our programs, and Kroger donates a percentage of your purchases.

Want to go above and beyond? Make a direct, secure donation of any amount via Credit, Debit, or PayPal.
