About the FAA

A parent-run organization built on one belief: that every student at Northview deserves the chance to become an artist.

Our Mission

The Northview HS Fine Arts Association exists to close the gap between what school budgets provide and what exceptional arts programs actually need. We believe fine arts are not a luxury — they are an essential part of a complete education.

Through parent membership, volunteerism, and community fundraising, we ensure that student artists in Art, Band, Chorus, Drama, and Orchestra have the instruments, materials, costumes, and opportunities they deserve.

We are proudly a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All contributions are tax-deductible, and we are fully accountable to our members and the Northview community.

Our Story

The Northview Fine Arts Association was founded by a small group of parents who saw something extraordinary happening in the school's arts programs — and wanted to make sure it kept happening. The five programs were already producing talented, dedicated young artists. But tight school budgets meant unmet needs: aging instruments, worn costumes, registration fees that some families couldn't afford.

Those founding parents organized, filed for nonprofit status, and started showing up — at football games running concession stands, at meetings making the case for why the arts matter. Year by year, the FAA grew. More parents joined, more programs received support, and more students got the chance to perform, create, and compete at the highest levels.

Today, the FAA supports over 900 students across five programs and is one of the most active parent organizations at Northview High School. None of it happens without parents like you.

What We Stand For

Student-Centered

Every decision we make starts and ends with what is best for student artists. Programs come first.

Community-Driven

We are a volunteer organization. Our strength comes from parents showing up for one another's kids.

Transparent

We are committed to showing our members exactly how their donations are used. All members are welcome at board meetings.

Inclusive

We support all five programs equally. Whether your student is in Art or Orchestra, they matter equally to the FAA.
