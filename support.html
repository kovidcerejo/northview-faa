Two Easy Ways to Give

Whether you shop at Kroger or want to make a direct gift, every contribution helps keep the fine arts thriving at Northview.

Kroger Community Rewards

If you shop at Kroger, you can support the FAA every time you check out — at absolutely no cost to you. Just link your Kroger Plus card once and Kroger donates on your behalf.

1

Create or Sign In to Your Kroger Account

Visit krogercommunityrewards.com and sign in with your Kroger account, or create a free account if you don't have one.

2

Search for Northview FAA Programs

Under 'Community Rewards', search for any of the five Northview programs by name or by their code (listed below).

3

Select Your Program(s)

Select the program(s) you want to support. You can link multiple programs to support all the arts at once.

4

Shop as Usual

That's it! Every qualifying Kroger purchase you make will now earn a donation to the FAA at no extra cost to you.

Kroger Rewards FAQ

Does it cost me anything?

No — Kroger Community Rewards is completely free for you. Kroger donates a percentage of your purchases from their own funds. You pay the same price as always.

How much does Kroger donate?

Kroger donates a percentage of eligible purchases, which varies by period. The more FAA members who link their cards, the more the program earns.

Can I link more than one program?

Yes! You can link all five Northview programs — Art, Band, Chorus, Drama, and Orchestra — so all programs benefit from your purchases.

Do I need to renew my Kroger link?

Yes, Kroger Community Rewards must be re-enrolled each year. You'll receive a reminder from Kroger, or you can check your account settings anytime.
